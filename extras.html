PROJECT: Toaster_MK3 // STATUS: FAILURE

Attempted to integrate a micro-Vithium cell into a standard kitchen toaster. Hypothesis: Could achieve perfect toast crispiness in 0.8 seconds. Result: Toaster achieved sentience for 4 seconds, screamed in binary, then vaporized itself and three feet of countertop. Conclusion: Consumer-grade appliances are not ready for Vithium. Also, Phenn is mad about the counter.

ANALYSIS: VeritasOS_v8.1 // STATUS: INSECURE

Lyra bypassed their latest security patch in under 15 minutes using a script disguised as a cat GIF. The entire corporate network is held together by the digital equivalent of duct tape and a single underpaid intern's password from three years ago ("password123"). It's not even a challenge anymore. It's just sad.

PROJECT: C.O.R.E._FIRMWARE_v3.5 // STATUS: NOMINAL

Patched the sensitivity module to differentiate between combat-induced tachycardia and... 'other' cardiac spikes. The system no longer flags a 'Code Red' every time Phenn wears that one specific sweater. Unintended side effect: The C.O.R.E. now uses 0.01% of its processing power to generate a low-frequency purr when idle. I'm calling it a feature.

ANALYSIS: Veritas "Data Scrubber" // STATUS: HILARIOUS

Mae and I reverse-engineered one of Veritas's portable data-wiping devices. We were expecting military-grade encryption erasure. Instead, it just reformats the target drive and replaces every file with a 10-hour loop of Rick Didn't Askley presenting a PowerPoint on synergy. It's not a weapon; it's psychological warfare.
