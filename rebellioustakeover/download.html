Installation Guide
(for Non-Steam players)


// It should be easy enough if you've ever played an indie game before.

1. Click one of the download buttons above to get the game's .zip file.

2. Find the downloaded file on your computer and extract its contents to a new folder. Don't run it from inside the .zip!

3. Open the new folder and run RebelliousTakeover.exe.

4. If Windows gives you a warning, click "More info" and then "Run anyway". We promise, we're not a virus - It's just that Windows doesn't recognise us. Run the game through VirusTotal or your on-system antivirus if you don't believe us.

5. Enjoy the game!

DLC Installation

// Got some extra content? Here's how to plug it in.

1. Download the DLC, which will be a single .pak file.

2. Navigate to where you extracted the main game. You should see the RebelliousTakeover.exe file.

3. Follow this path: RebelliousTakeover/Content/Paks/. The exact name of the first folder might vary, but you're looking for the 'Content/Paks' subfolders.

4. Drag and drop your downloaded .pak file into the RebelliousTakeover/Content/Paks/ folder.

5. That's it! Launch the game, and the new content should be active.

It's a hybrid-genre game that blends three core elements:
- Beat-'Em-Up Combat: Fast-paced, high-octane action.
- Visual Novel Storytelling: A deep, character-driven slice-of-life story where you build relationships.
- Semi Open-World Exploration: Discover side-stories and encounters in the city of Nexon.

The story follows a small group of rebels trying to take down the monolithic corporation, Veritas Industries. The main conflict kicks off when Veritas raids an old hideout belonging to the team's leader, Deren, and steals the blueprints to his revolutionary invention: The C.O.R.E, a Vithium-powered heart replacement. The team must fight to get the blueprints back before Veritas can turn the life-saving technology into a weapon for corporate control.

At its heart, the game explores themes of anti-corporatism, found family, and neurodiversity. Most of the main characters are on the autistic spectrum and are simply trying to live their lives to the best of their ability. The story focuses on how this group of outcasts finds connection and fights for a better future in a world that wants to exploit them.
