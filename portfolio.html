Professional Journey

A brief overview of my experience and education.

Founder & Lead at Dev4Fun Studios

2021 - 2025

Founded and led a remote team on the Roblox platform, focusing on project management and cultivating talent to build ambitious fighting games like Revolture's Trigger. This role provided valuable experience in leading a development team from concept to release.

Level 2 Creative Media (Games Development)

Academic Time: September 2022 to July 2023

Achieved a Merit in my Creative Media Skills certificate at Loughborough College. This course, alongside foundational GCSEs, provided the essential building blocks for my technical and creative career.

Level 3 Creative Media (Games Development)

Acedemic Time: September 2024 - July 2026

As of writing, I am currently in the second and final year of my advanced course at Burton & South Derbyshire College, specializing entirely in games development. The curriculum is focused on practical skills in Unreal Engine, 3D modeling, and core design principles for the modern games industry.

Founder & Lead at vecShift Studios

2025 - Present

While undertaking my Level 3 studies, I founded my own indie studio to develop my flagship title, Rebellious Takeover, and help with publishing smaller college-related Unreal projects like Goober Origins: Electric Gooberloo. I manage all aspects of development, applying my academic knowledge directly to a real-world, large-scale project.
